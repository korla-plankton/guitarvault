## Full Description

GuitarVault is the ultimate collection manager for guitar enthusiasts. Catalog every instrument with deep specifications, capture photos with on-device AI background removal, track valuations and insurance, and fill in your knowledge one spec at a time with the Daily Spec Challenge.

🎸 DEEP SPECIFICATIONS
60+ fields per guitar: body, neck and fretboard woods, scale length, neck profile, fret size, pickups, electronics, hardware, tuning machines, finish, serial number, strings, and more. Don't see a field you need? Add your own custom fields.

📸 PHOTO CAPTURE + AI
Take photos with CameraX or paste reference images from your browser. The magic wand icon runs ML Kit on-device AI to remove backgrounds from any photo — with undo if it doesn't get it right. Full-screen viewer with pinch-to-zoom and swipe between photos.

💰 VALUATION & INSURANCE
Track purchase price, current value, and estimated value with full history. Record insurance details: provider, policy number, coverage type, deductible. See gain/loss at a glance.

🔧 CONDITION & MAINTENANCE
Rate your guitar's condition from Mint to Poor. Log every string change, setup, repair, refret, and electronics work with cost and technician details.

🎲 DAILY SPEC CHALLENGE
The app picks a random guitar and asks you to fill in one missing spec — gamify your data entry! Track your collection completeness with a progress bar.

🔍 SPEC SEARCH
Search Google, Reverb, and eBay for guitar specifications with one tap. Find the specs online, then fill them in.

📋 WISHLIST, OWNED & SOLD
Unified status tabs let you manage guitars you own, wish to acquire, and have sold — all in one place. Acquire a wishlist guitar with one tap and record the purchase price.

✨ MATERIAL YOU
Adaptive dynamic color that matches your device theme. Dark and light modes.

🔒 100% PRIVATE
All data is stored locally on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking. Your collection never leaves your phone unless you export it.
